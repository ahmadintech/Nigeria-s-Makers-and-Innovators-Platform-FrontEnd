About Growth Ventures

Growth Ventures is a venture capital firm focused on early-stage investments in innovative Nigerian startups. We look for companies with strong founding teams, scalable business models, and a clear path to profitability. Our areas of interest include Agri-Tech, FinTech, and Renewable Energy, with a focus on Pre-seed, Seed, and Series A stages. We provide not only capital but also strategic guidance and access to our network of industry experts to help our portfolio companies thrive and achieve their full potential.
